# Runbook: GitHub 2FA recovery

**Applies to:** Engineers locked out of GitHub due to a lost 2FA device.

## Symptom
You replaced your phone or lost your authenticator and can't pass GitHub's 2FA prompt.

## Recovery
1. Try your saved **recovery codes** first (generated when you enabled 2FA). One code works once.
2. If you have no recovery codes, you cannot self-recover. Open a ticket with the **IT Identity** team from a verified corp device.
3. IT verifies your identity over a video call, then resets 2FA on the org-managed account.
4. Re-enroll a new authenticator and **download fresh recovery codes** immediately.

## Prevention
Store recovery codes in the corp password manager, not on the device that runs the authenticator. Never share recovery codes in a ticket or chat — IT will reset 2FA without them.
